Community Guidelines

Last updated May 11, 2026
Thoughts is built for open discussions, opinions, and public interaction through text-based posts and voting. These guidelines exist to help keep the platform respectful, safe, and enjoyable for everyone.

Be Respectful

Treat other users with respect. Healthy debates and disagreements are allowed, but harassment, bullying, threats, hate speech, or abusive behavior are not permitted.

  • No personal attacks or targeted harassment
  • No hate speech based on race, religion, nationality, gender, sexual orientation, or disability
  • No threats of violence or intimidation
  • No encouragement of self-harm or harmful behavior

Keep Content Appropriate

Thoughts is a text-based social platform. Users may post opinions, discussions, and polls, but content must remain appropriate for a broad audience.

  • No sexually explicit content
  • No graphic violence or disturbing content
  • No promotion of illegal activities
  • No spam, scams, or misleading content
  • No impersonation of individuals or organizations

Spam and Manipulation

Do not misuse the platform through spam or artificial engagement tactics.

  • No mass posting or repetitive content
  • No fake engagement or vote manipulation
  • No misleading polls created to deceive users
  • No automated activity or bots without permission

Privacy and Personal Information

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share private or confidential information without permission.

  • No posting phone numbers, addresses, passwords, or financial information
  • No sharing private conversations without consent
  • No doxxing or exposing personal information

False Information and Harmful Content

Users should avoid spreading intentionally misleading or harmful information.

  • No coordinated misinformation campaigns
  • No fraudulent claims or impersonation
  • No harmful medical, legal, or financial misinformation intended to deceive

Platform Enforcement

We may remove content or suspend accounts that violate these guidelines or harm the platform experience.

  • Content may be removed without notice
  • Repeated violations may lead to account suspension or permanent bans
  • Severe violations may be reported to relevant authorities where required by law

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it through the platform or contact support.

Email: thoughtsapp.support@gmail.com

Our Goal

Thoughts is designed to encourage meaningful discussions, opinions, and public interaction. We want the platform to remain open while ensuring it stays respectful, safe, and constructive for everyone.
