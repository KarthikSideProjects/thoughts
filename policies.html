1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using Thoughts, including while registering, creating polls, interacting with users, or contacting support.

Information You May Provide

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password
  • Contact preferences
  • Authentication information

Sensitive Information

We do not process sensitive personal information.

Social Login Information

You may choose to sign in using services such as Facebook or X. In those cases, we may receive profile information from the social login provider.

Application Data

If you use our application, we may collect application-related information such as push notification permissions and usage-related analytics.

This information helps us maintain platform security, troubleshoot issues, and improve user experience.

2. How We Process Your Information

We process personal information to operate, improve, and secure the platform.

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users
  • Deliver platform services
  • Provide customer support
  • Send updates regarding policies or services
  • Enable user interactions and communication
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve user experience and platform performance

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location, we process your information under various legal bases including:

  • User consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Protection of vital interests

Users located in regions such as the European Union, United Kingdom, Canada, and other jurisdictions may have additional legal rights under applicable privacy laws.

4. Sharing Personal Information

We may share information in limited situations including:

  • Business mergers or acquisitions

Content you post publicly on Thoughts — including polls, comments, usernames, and interactions — may be visible to other users and publicly accessible.

5. Social Logins

If you choose to log in using a third-party social media account, we may receive profile information such as your name, email address, and profile image depending on the provider and your privacy settings.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of third-party providers to understand how they handle your information.
